Biography

Arijit Singh is an Indian singer and music composer who is known for his wide vocal range and soulful voice. He was born on April 25, 1987, in Jiaganj, Murshidabad, West Bengal, India. Arijit started his career in television, where he appeared as a contestant on the singing reality show "Fame Gurukul" in 2005. He finished the show in fourth place, but he gained a lot of popularity for his singing talent.

Arijit's first major Bollywood break came in 2011, when he sang the song "Raabta" for the film "Agent Vinod". The song was a huge hit, and it helped Arijit to establish himself as one of the leading singers in the Hindi film industry.
